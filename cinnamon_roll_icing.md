# Cinnamon Roll Icing

**Type:** Ingredient
**Serves:** 12 w/ preparation of Cinnamon Rolls

## Ingredients:
- 2 Cups Powdered Sugar
- 2 Tablespoons Melted Butter
- $\frac14$ Cup Milk
- 2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

## Steps:
1. Combine ingredients in a bowl and beat until smooth.
