# Easy Bavarian Cream

**Type:** Dessert Filling\
**Makes:** 3 Cups

## Ingredients:
- 3.4 Ounce Box Instant Vanilla Pudding
- $1\frac{1}{2}$ Cups Heavy Whipping Cream

## Steps:
1. Whisk together for about 5 minutes or until light and fluffy.
