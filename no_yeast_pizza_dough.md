# No-Yeast Pizza Dough

**Type:** Ingredient
**Serves:** ?

## Ingredients:
- 1$\frac{1}{3}$ Cup Flour
- 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
- $\frac{1}{2}$ Teaspoon Salt
- $\frac{1}{2}$ Cup Milk
- 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil

## Steps:
1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Top and bake at 450$^\circ$F for 8 to 10 minutes.
